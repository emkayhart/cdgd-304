Sweet Potato Fries and a Moscow Mule

An easy side dish and my favorite drink.

Fry Ingredients:

  • 4 large sweet potatoes
  • Brown sugar
  • Steak rub or Salt/Pepper
  • Choice of oil (Olive, vegetable, etc)
  • Medium/large mixing bowl
  • Baking sheet + parchment/wax paper

Drink Ingredients:

  • Tito's Vodka
  • Reed's Ginger Beer (12oz)
  • The juice of 1 large lime + ¼ lime wedge
  • Ice
  • Copper Mug (if available)

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Fry Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 360Â° F
  2. Peel sweet potatoes
  3. Cut sweet potatoes into chunky strips
  4. Add strips to mixing bowl
  5. Coat potatoes with oil; sprinkle seasoning and brown sugar to desired amount
  6. Mix with hands, or spatula
  7. Spread potatoes out on baking sheet
  8. Bake/broil for 30 minutes

While the potatoes are in the oven, make your Moscow Mule! ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Drink Intructions:

  1. In your preferred cup, add 3 or 4 ice large cubes
  2. Add a shot (or two) of Tito's Vodka
  3. Add ½ of a Reed's Ginger Beer
  4. Juice a lime
  5. Stir (or shake with a cocktail shaker)
  6. Garnish with lime wedge
